Out of Pocket

Welcome!

This is a web app geared towards teenagers and young adults. The app helps people establish a budget, track their money, and learn about finance. This isn’t a real banking app, no real money is used, all deposits and withdrawals will be controlled by the user. The user has to put the amount of money they want into an account and subtract the money as they go.
